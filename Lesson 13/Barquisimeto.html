Economy

The location of Barquisimeto in the interior of Venezuela, allows it to be a confluence of land routes between the central and western regions, with the western plains and the south, with the coastal axis to the north, with the Andes to the west and with Zulia to the northwest. In addition, it is located next to a fertile valley, which has contributed to agricultural production, the development of commerce, service and the manufacturing industry, which are the main economic activities of the urban area
Since 1983 there has been in the Industrial Zone III of Barquisimeto the largest wholesale market in Venezuela, known as MERCABAR (Mercado Mayorista de Alimentos de Barquisimeto), which has a land of 108 hectares (42 of which are developed), whose activity The main one is the production of public services, intended for sale and whose income comes from this activity. One of its main objectives is to provide, maintain and manage adequate spaces for the organization of wholesale trade in its area of ​​influence. The city has a Nominal GDP of USD 10,750 million and a nominal GDP per capita of USD 8,552, which represents a PPP GDP per capita of 14,810, 17 being the 5th city in economic activity.

Culture

Barquisimeto is the seat for mass celebrations, such as: The International Carnivals of Barquisimeto (February-March), where a great variety of floats are observed, and musical artistic shows of recognized trajectory, The International Fair of Barquisimeto which begins on 14 September (anniversary of the founding of Barquisimeto), and ends on September 25 of each year, where you can see, among many things, various exhibitions and musical shows, and finally, a wide range of options to enjoy Christmas in Barquisimeto , in which you can see various exhibitions, musical shows and many fireworks. It is customary to receive the New Year in La Flor de Venezuela, with family and friends.

Vacations

Barquisimeto has several natural reserves that allow visitors to enjoy excellent experiences and vacations, due to its geographical location and wealth of natural resources, we can find rivers with the wine waterfall, mountains such as Cubiro, Las Mayitas, among others, in addition to having an excellent service in the city in hotels, inns and restaurants.
