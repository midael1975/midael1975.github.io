Purpose

The chamber of commerce aims to promote competition and improve productivity in the commerce sector, within the territory of Lara state. The chamber provides support through financial aid, research, sector information and consumption statistics. At the same time it tries to establish quality norms and work standards. It also helps entrepreneurs to resolve conflicts, training, support and strengthening of companies and thus strengthen the economic activity of the state.
