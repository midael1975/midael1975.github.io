Target Audience

Description

The website is aimed at people 12 years and older, who live in Venezuela or wish to visit this beautiful country, who can access the internet to visit the website, the weather is something that affects everyone but especially those who plan to do travel by different means, whether by land, air or sea, for vacations or work, farmers, and anyone who the state of the weather may affect their performance or safety

Persons

Name:Javier Bastidass

Age:thirties

Ocupation:Farmer

Purpose of visit:Javier would visit our site to find out about the weather predictions to make decisions on the preservation of his crops or when is the right time to cultivate and thus make his work profitable and prosperous.

Name:Pedro y Maria Velasquez

Age:twenties

Ocupation:Web Designer

Purpose of visit:Pedro and Maria would visit our website to find out if the places they want to visit for outdoor activities like camping have the weather conditions for them to visit and if the road conditions are optimal.

Scenarios

The scenarios that visitors can find solutions on this website are as follows:

The roads are safe?

Is there an emergency that is approaching or is it occurring?

What precautions should I take according to the weather reports?

Is it possible that the weather could affect my work?

Are the places we plan to go on vacation safe for the scheduled date?
